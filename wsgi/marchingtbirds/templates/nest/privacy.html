Marchingtbirds.tk Privacy Policy

Marchingtbirds.tk is the official website for the Mahwah High School Marching Thunderbirds Band, Mahwah, NJ. We know that it is important to protect the personal information of our users, and we take your privacy very seriously. By visiting our site, you are accepting the practices outlined in the policy below. If you are unsatisfied with the manner in which we collect data or do not wish to have your data collected by our site, please do not use this site.

This privacy policy covers Marchingtbird.tk’s treatment of personal information that is gathered when you are on our site and when you use our services. This policy does not apply to the practices of third parties that Marchingtbirds.tk does not own or control.

1. Information collected by Marchingtbirds.tk

We only collect personal information that is relevant to the purpose of our website and the Marching Thunderbirds Band. We collect the following types of information from our users:

1.1. Information you provide to us

We receive and store data you provide to us on our servers. This data includes your username, password, and other information you submit while filling out our user registration form. It also includes the data you submit when filling out your user profile, which is necessary for you or your student’s participation in the Marching Thunderbirds Band. Some of this information is required to be gathered by law and other information helps us to better ensure the safety of you or your student. You can choose not to provide us with personal information, but this may limit you or your child’s ability to participate in the Marching Thunderbirds Band. If you do not wish to provide this information via our website, paper copies of our registration form can be made available.

1.2. Information we provide to you

One of the functions we provide on our website, if uniform captains and parents choose to use the feature, is information about the student’s uniform. This information is stored along with your other data on our server.

1.3. Automatic information

Marchingtbirds.tk uses Google Analytics, a third party service, to collect visitor data and analyze traffic on our site. This information helps us to manage our site traffic and better meet the needs of our users. When you visit our site, the pages you view and a piece of data called a “cookie” are downloaded to your computer. The cookie is used to store information about you but does not contain personal details besides information about your computer, IP address, and geographic location. This information is sent to Google Analytics and used for traffic analysis only. Depending on the browser you use, you can set your preferences to block/refuse cookies or notify you before they are downloaded. See their terms of service for more information.

2. E-mail communications

Though Marchingtbirds.tk collects e-mail address data from you and your student, this data will never be used to send you or your student e-mail other than the communications you receive from the Marching Thunderbirds staff and officers. If you receive an e-mail from our website that did not come from the staff or officers, it is likely that this is a malicious e-mail and should be reported to the staff immediately. Please do not open or click links in these e-mails, as they can lead to the compromising of your account. Delete the email immediately.

3. Sharing your information

The information you provide to Marchingtbirds.tk will never be shared with third parties under any circumstances - ever. However, it will be visible internally to our staff, uniform volunteers and captains, and our web design team:

3.1. Marching band staff

All personal information you provide will be available to the marching band staff. This information is necessary for the proper functioning of the band and for creating a safe environment for students. The information you provide through our site takes the place of paper forms you may have previously filled out.

3.2. Captains, parents, and other volunteers

When assigning you or your student their uniform parts, uniform parents and captains may have access to some information about you or your student. This information is limited to the student’s name, class year, instrument, and t-shirt size. We have taken security measures to ensure that all other information is hidden from these volunteers. If we discover that captains, parents, or other volunteers are attempting to circumvent these security measures, their accounts will be immediately and permanently suspended from our site.

3.3. Web design team

The web design team will have access to all personal information that you provide. This is necessary for the regular maintenance and upkeep of the site and to help staff members troubleshoot problems when they arise. The web design team is expressly forbidden from disclosing or using this information in any way outside the maintenance of the site.

4. Keeping information secure

Your Marchingtbirds.tk account information is protected by a password for your privacy and security. We are doing the best we can to keep your data secure, but it is equally important for you to pick a secure password that will adequately protect your data. In addition, make sure to log out immediately after using the site, especially if you are on a shared machine. If you feel that your account information or security has been compromised in any way, please contact the Marchingtbirds.tk web staff immediately.

5. Children under 18 years of age

We expect children under 18 to be using Marchingtbirds.tk, which makes sense considering most students in the band are under 18. As mentioned earlier, we will never share or disclose information about you or your children under any circumstances except to the parties disclosed in section 3. Though we cannot realistically enforce it, we recommend that children under 18 only use our site with their parent’s permission.

6. Indemnification

Marchingtbirds.tk makes the best effort possible to protect your data but we cannot guarantee that our security measures will not be compromised. By using our site, you agree that Marchingtbirds.tk cannot be held liable in any way for the loss, theft, or unauthorized access or usage of your data specified in section 1. Additionally, you agree that in no event shall Marchingtbirds.tk, the marching band staff, marching band volunteers, or the web design team be liable under contract, tort, strict liability, negligence or other legal theory for any damages, even if either party knew or should have known the possibility thereof.

7. Changes to the privacy policy

Marchingtbirds.tk reserves the right to amend and change our privacy policy at any time at its sole discretion. Use of the information we collect is subject to the privacy policy in effect at the time such information is used. If we make changes to the privacy policy, we will notify you by posting an announcement to the Marchingtbirds.tk website so you are aware of our policies.

8. Effective date of this privacy policy

This privacy policy is effective as of January 1, 2015.
